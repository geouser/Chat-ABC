Learn English, Anywhere, Anytime!

Our unique application and English teaching method lets the students learn English in a fun and effective way that keeps the students engaged and challenged. Finish all FOUR activities and move on to the next level. Follow your progress on your profile.

Step 1: Play the game – Fun and interactive word memory game for each lesson
Step 2: Watch the video – Listen, speak and read while watching the lesson video
Step 3: Take the quiz – Once you feel prepared, take the lesson quiz.
Step 4: Schedule Tutorial – Student can choose, Live teacher, Live webinar or recorded webinar

Wechat Integration & Lesson Tutorial

Chatchat abc uses an API with Wechat, that will connect your Wechat account to your account at Chatchat abc. This makes it easy for you to connect with our English tutors for lesson tutorial and for easy payment processing. We have 3 lesson tutorials and payment options.

1 on 1 Live Tutorial – Students will have a live 1 on 1 tutorial with one of our many tutors using Wechat video and audio. This option costs 20 ¥ and is the recommended method of lesson tutorial.
Live Webinar Tutorial – Students can join a scheduled live webinar tutorial. For this option, we use GoT0Webinar, which allows students to train and participate directly from mobile devices. This option costs 10 ¥ and is our students favorite method of lesson tutorial.
Recorded Webinar Tutorial – Students can join a recorded webinar tutorial anytime and is our most affordable lesson tutorial at only 5 ¥. This method is not recommended but is an effective and affordable alternative for students.
