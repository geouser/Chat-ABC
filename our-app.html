The more you PLAY, the more English you learn!

We designed and developed the Chatchat abc app to make learning English fun and easy. Our powerful and user friendly app is loaded with cool features. Play the game, watch the video, take the quiz and you’re now ready for your lesson tutorial.

User Home Page

Visit the user profile page to update your user information and view the badges that you have earned. Make sure that you keep your status, tagline, location and email address up to date, so that our staff will know how you’re doing!

User Videos Page

Check out your completed lesson videos in your user video section. Here you can watch the video, read the lesson captions in English and Chinese and practice speaking the words along with our talented English speaking actors. Remember, practice makes perfect and this section is very essential to your Chatchat abc English learning experience!

User Word Bank

Want to review all the words you have learned? You can find them in the user word bank tab on the home page. Refresh your memory of the spelling and listen to how the word sounds by clicking on the play icon. Make sure your practice often to refresh your memory and review all the words that you have already learned from previously completed lessons.

User Profile Page

On the user home page is where you can find all of the cool features like adding your favorite tutor or even viewing your favorite tutors schedule. You can review completed lesson videos and review words that you have already learned in the Word Dictionary section or get help in the FAQ tab.
